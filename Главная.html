Miss Dior Rose N'Roses (Pahnet Dior)

The freshness of an armful of colorful fresh petals, the invigorating energy of citrusy dew. Miss Dior Rose N'Roses (Pahnet Dior) is a sparkling floral fragrance, a profusion of Grasse Roses refreshed by a zest of Bergamot and heightened by the intensity of a White Musk note.

Vibrant essence of Geranium refines the composition with its lemony aromas to faithfully recreate the irresistible scent of an endless field of flowers. A bright pink hue becomes a fragrance that takes hold of you and never lets go.
